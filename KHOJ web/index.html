what is KHOJ

KHOJ

KHOJ is a creative event organised by IIT Kharagpur's Azad Hall of Residence, encapsulates the spirit of a warm welcome and a smooth transition for arriving freshmen. Khoj is a thrilling experience for students throughout the vital early days of their university adventure, designed to acquaint them with the large campus and the vibrant academic atmosphere. Khoj encourages freshmen to make relationships with fellow students, staff members, and the myriad of resources available, with a focus on developing a sense of community.

    KEY FEATURES

  • Campus Tour
  • Departmental Introduction
  • Interactive Session
  • Resource Guidance
  • Student Organizations
  • Cultural & Social Events
  • Networking Opportunity: Khoj provides a platform for new students to start building thier network by interacting with faculty members and senior students. Khoj is an opportunity for freshmen to feel comfortable , informed about their acadamic environment . It amis to ease their transition
